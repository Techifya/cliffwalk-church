What We Do

Many ways to connect, grow and serve together throughout the week

Sunday Services

Join us for service every Sunday. Together we worship God led by gifted musicians, and look at what the Bible has to say about how we should live each day to please God.

Those aged 3–16 usually spend some of their time in separate groups. There is also a crèche room.

Sunday 11:00 AM English Service
Sunday 6:30 PM Portuguese Service

Home Groups

Join us for Home Groups where we gather together for prayer, Bible study and fellowship. The last Friday of each month we all meet together at the Church.

Wednesday Evening English Home Group
Friday Evening Portuguese Home Group

Contact us for locations.

Coffee Morning

We offer free coffee, tea, fruit, biscuits and cakes — but we also aim to offer an ear to listen, a heart to love and a Word that Saves! Just as our Lord Jesus has commanded us to do! Be part of this...

Saturday 10:30 – 11:30 AM Outside the Church

Youth Ministry

Our aim is to Grow, Honour, Reach, Connect and Discover the love of God for the youth.

More Ways to Connect

Women's Ministry

Started in 2009 as a small group of women who saw the need to gather and pray for the families and ministries of the Lord's work.

Learn more →

Men's Ministry

We meet together to pray for each other at 7pm on the last Friday of each month. We also run events such as a men's breakfast about once every 3 months.

Mission's Ministry

We have a passion for obeying God's Great Commission through financial and spiritual support to our missionaries around the world and making a positive impact in our community.

Daytime Fun & Fellowship

We meet every first Thursday of the month at 2pm for an afternoon of fellowship at Cliff Walk Church.

Other Activities

During the year we do many different things to get together and to share God's love with others. These include BBQs, fun afternoons, music concerts, bazaars and talent evenings.

Details are posted on our Facebook page.

Everyone is welcome

Come along to any event or get in touch. We'd love to meet you.
