Welcome

Come as you are

We are a group of Christians from many nationalities — 20 at the last count — who have committed together to love God and serve Canning Town.

You are very welcome to come along to any event or to get in touch by phone or email. Our Sunday services are also live streamed on our Facebook page.

Other activities of prayer, Bible study and fellowship take place during the week. Details are broadcast on Facebook each week or email cliffwalkchurch@hotmail.co.uk. The Sunday teaching can also be viewed on the church's YouTube channel.
